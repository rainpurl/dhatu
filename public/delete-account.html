Delete your Dhātu account

Dhātu is a free app for learning Gujarati, published by the Dalwadi Foundation. This page explains how to delete your account and the data associated with it.

Delete it yourself, in the app (fastest)

If you have the app installed, you can permanently delete your account and all associated data right away:

  1. Open Dhātu and go to the Profile tab.
  2. Scroll down and tap Delete account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and data are removed immediately.

Request deletion without the app

If you have uninstalled the app or cannot sign in, email us from the email address on your account and we will delete it for you:

Email a deletion request

Send to dhatulearning@katr.es with the subject "Delete my account." We remove the account within 30 days of verifying the request.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes all of the following, and it cannot be undone:

  • Your account and sign-in (name, email, and account identifier).
  • Your chosen username.
  • Your learning progress: lessons completed, streak, points ("Kaudi"), and saved words.
  • Your social connections: people you follow and any pokes sent or received.
  • Your public profile.

Data retention

Once deleted, none of the above is retained, except where we are required to keep limited records by law. Pronunciation practice audio is never stored in the first place: clips are transcribed transiently to give you feedback and are not saved, so there is nothing to delete.
