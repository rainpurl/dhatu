Dhātu Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026. Dhātu is a free app for learning Gujarati.

The short version

Dhātu asks you to sign in with Google so it can save your learning progress and let you connect with friends. We collect only what is needed to run the app. We do not sell your data, we do not show ads, and we do not share your information with anyone except the services that store it for us (Google Firebase). You can ask us to delete your account and data at any time.

What we collect

  • Account info from Google sign-in: your name, email address, profile photo, and a Google account identifier.
  • A username that you choose in the app.
  • Your learning progress: lessons completed, streak, points ("Kaudi"), words saved for review, and similar in-app activity.
  • Social connections you create in the app: people you follow and "pokes" you send or receive.
  • Short voice recordings for pronunciation practice (optional): when you choose to practice speaking, the app records a brief audio clip while you hold the microphone button. To score your pronunciation, that clip may be sent to a speech-to-text service that converts it to text. The clip is processed to produce the transcript and is not stored by us after that; it is used only to give you feedback, never for advertising. If speech recognition is unavailable, the app instead asks you to confirm you said the phrase, and no audio leaves your device.

We do not collect your location, contacts, or photos, and we do not record audio except during pronunciation practice as described above. We do not collect any other sensitive personal data.

How we use it

  • To sign you in and keep you signed in.
  • To save and sync your progress across your devices.
  • To show your public profile (username, name, photo, streak, points) to friends you connect with.

Where it is stored

Your account and progress are stored using Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore), operated by Google. Their handling of that data is governed by Google's privacy policy. Lesson audio and images are delivered as ordinary web content. Pronunciation audio clips are processed by a speech-to-text service (Cloudflare Workers AI) only to produce a transcript for feedback, and are not retained.

Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data, and we do not share it with advertisers or data brokers. The third parties involved are Google Firebase, which stores your account and progress on our behalf, and the speech-to-text service that transcribes pronunciation clips on our behalf. Information you choose to make visible to friends (your public profile) is shared with those friends within the app.

Your choices and deletion

You can sign out at any time from the Profile tab. You can also permanently delete your account and all associated data directly in the app: Profile tab → Delete account. This removes your account, username, learning progress, and social connections and cannot be undone. If you prefer, you can instead email us at the address below and we will remove it.

Children

Dhātu is a general-audience educational app and is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and its date.
